About RescueBites


RescueBites is a pioneering initiative dedicated to tackling the pressing issue of food waste while simultaneously alleviating hunger. Our mission is to bridge the gap between surplus food providers, such as restaurants or mess with the local NGOs that serve those in need. By facilitating the efficient redistribution of excess food, we ensure that perfectly good, nutritious food is not wasted but instead reaches the tables of individuals and families facing food insecurity. Our platform leverages technology to streamline the process, making it easy for food donors to connect with charities and community organizations. At SurplusShare, we believe in creating a sustainable food system where surplus is shared and no one goes hungry. Join us in our mission to reduce food waste and nourish our communities.

We provide Surplus

We specialize in providing surplus food, ensuring that excess quantities which would otherwise go to waste are redirected to those in need. By collaborating with various organizations and initiatives, we distribute this surplus food to communities and individuals who can benefit from it most. Our mission is to minimize food waste while addressing hunger and food insecurity, fostering a more sustainable and compassionate society.


We maintain Hygiene

We prioritize hygiene and safety in all our food handling processes. We ensure that all surplus food donations comply with strict sanitation standards, from pickup to delivery. Our partners and volunteers are trained in proper food safety protocols to guarantee that the food reaching those in need is fresh and safe.
